How to Earn — DTech Rewards

Quick summary (read this first)

  • You earn by completing tasks in the Mission Dashboard.
  • Each completed task adds to your Pending Balance.
  • Pending earnings are verified and moved to your Approved Balance (withdrawable) periodically.
  • Start with the Daily Boost (Tier 1) for the highest rewards.

The Tier System

Tasks are organized into Tiers. Completing higher tiers unlocks the next ones.

Tier 1 — Daily Boost

  • Status: Available daily.
  • Reward: High Yield (R0.10 — R0.30).
  • Goal: Complete all Popunder, In-Page, and Direct Link tasks.
  • Cooldown: 60 minutes after completion.

Tier 2 — Standard

  • Status: Unlocks when Tier 1 is on cooldown.
  • Reward: Medium Yield.
  • Cooldown: 20 minutes after completion.

Tier 3 — The Grind

  • Status: Always available when Tiers 1 & 2 are cooling down.
  • Reward: Low Yield (Unlimited).
  • Goal: Keep earning while waiting for high-yield tasks to refresh.

How to Complete Tasks

  • Pop-Under / Direct Link: Click the task. A new tab will open with the ad. Keep the tab open and wait for the timer on the dashboard (15s) to claim your reward.
  • In-Page: Click the task to view the ad page. Wait for the timer (15s) and claim.

Note: Closing the ad too quickly or using ad-blockers will prevent the reward from being claimed.

Pending vs Approved Balance

We use two balances to ensure fairness:

Pending Balance

Estimated earnings from your tasks. Not yet withdrawable.

Approved Balance

Verified earnings that can be withdrawn. Admin reviews and approves pending amounts based on actual ad revenue.

Withdrawals

  • Withdrawals are paid from your Approved Balance.
  • Fees apply based on the method (Airtime: 25%, Voucher: 30%, Cash Send: 40%).
  • Certificates are generated for every withdrawal request for transparency.

Rules

  • No VPNs or Proxies.
  • No Automation / Bots.
  • One account per user.
  • Ad-blockers must be disabled to earn.
